Introduction

Hi everyone! My name is Katie, I am a teacher and social entrepreneur from the UK. I am so excited to join this course to learn more about web development. I have already built a few simple websites using WordPress, including:

  • Abridge Academy - offering online courses for my social enterprise
  • Abridge EduCamp - a TEFL summer camp application platform
  • STEM Boost - a membership site for science teachers and parents
  • Travel Boost - a travel blog I've mostly forgotten about...
I hope to improve my web development skills in order to work on a variety of EdTech projects, helping to open up STEM and language learning to students from around the world. I would love to learn how to build websites from scratch to bring these ideas to reality.
